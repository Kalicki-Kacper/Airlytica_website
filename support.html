Technical support & contact

We want Airlytica to work reliably and be truly useful for you. If something is not working as expected, please let us know – every report helps us improve the app.

  • Technical issues: describe the problem, your phone model, operating system version and – if possible – the steps to reproduce the bug.
  • Feature requests: if there is a feature you would like to see in Airlytica, feel free to suggest it in your message – we review all feedback.
  • General feedback: your input helps us decide what to improve next and how to make the app more helpful.
